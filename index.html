Our Story

A long time ago ....

It's no surprise to many that Sarah has been dubbed "Sally" for her obsession with food and particular nature. She's been known to order extra sauce, always on the side. It's a good thing that she came across her own "Harry" who can summarize in 55 seconds his love for her.

But it's hard to summarize a relationship that has spanned a third of our lives, and in true "When Harry Met Sally" form we got off to a slow start. The early years had some awesome highlights but also were sprinkled with very sporadic dates, some misunderstood relationship statuses (silly Facebook!) and even a six month break up.

Why do we bring this up on our wedding website? Because it's what made us who we are - unique individuals who were trying to figure out who we were while staying true to ourselves in our own, odd way.

And those early years are what made us stronger apart, but also brought us together into the couple we are today.

So what about the middle years? Well, we were busy building our careers, buying a house and just having fun being twenty-somethings.

And then thirty hit. And we STILL were not ready for marriage. We took some amazing adventures, both near and far. We spent twelve full days sightseeing across the pond and wanted to spend more time together. Shawn saw Sarah's magical "Disney Side" and didn't run.

Somewhere in that time we realized that our independence was a critical part of who we are, but that we are strongest when we are together. So we decided: Let's both be independent, together ...

And now where are we?

We're in the chaos of wedding planning (which isn't as fun as everyone says it is) and before we know it this time of transition will be a distant memory.

We are very excited for the day when we finally get to say "I Do" in front of all our friends and family, and start the next chapter of our lives together. The first step will be a really awesome party with delicious food and lots and lots of dancing. ☺

And then we will look back on all this and reflect.

And then we will look forward to continuing our story, and are glad you can be a part of it.

Accommodations & Activities

We have reserved two wedding blocks for Friday, September 1, 2017 through Monday, September 4, 2017 under the Scalia / Headrick wedding at the following hotels. Both are flexible and accommodating, so if you are interested in staying longer or shifting your stay to days outside these dates please contact us so we can put you in direct communication with the event coordinator. Although no rooms are blocked out for other days, if rooms are available they will honor the discounted rate.

Please alert us if you find the room you are interested in is unavailable and we can add more to the block as needed. There are three ways to get the wedding block price:

  1. Click the reservation link for the hotel you're interested in and you'll be taken to a landing page for our wedding group with the block price already entered.
  2. Contact the hotels by phone and provide the group codes listed.
  3. Book on the main Hilton or Marriott websites and, when offered, provide the appropriate group code. On the Hilton website the group code box is readily apparent, but on the Marriott website it's hidden on the hotel location detail page under the special rates option on the right-hand column.

The final day to book a reservation at the group rate is Friday, August 11, 2017.
